Industry Data

After comparing each ideal job, an investigation into Team Phoenix's required skillset was completed. Varied job descriptions and information found from other sources were collated to assess what skills were most needed from employers and industry professionals. This was completed for each job and the complete list can be found in Appendix A – D. That information was then compared to Burning Glass (2018) to determine the most needed skills compared to the overall technical and generic skill demand. This information can be found in tables 3 – 6.

Based on the above information all IT careers have similar generic skill requirements. Communication, including written, oral and interpersonal are necessary in all jobs. Organisational and general project management skills also ranked high. Both skill sets align with the collaborate nature of the IT industry. One distinction between the data analysts roles and the Network and software engineer roles was the emotive language used when comparing job listings and desired skills. As there is currently no recognised national shortage of the desired positions (National Skills Commission 2021) the technical skills required for the chosen jobs did not appear as high on the employment demand lists as the general skills.
