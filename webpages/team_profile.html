Meet The Team

To better understand how Team Phoenix would work with one another, 3 personality tests were completed by each member. These results are listed in table 1 and were shared when the group was formed. The differing personality types meant no two people would be the same and everyone would have their own place in the group without fear of being left out or clashing with any other members. It would also allow for a working environment that is similar to a professional setting where colleagues are vastly different.
