IT Technologies

There are a lot of fascinating developments going on in the IT world, many of which may fall by the wayside, but some of which are likely to change the way the world works. Historic examples of such developments include the Internet, smartphones, cloud computing and public-key cryptography.

Reports were made on the following 4 areas below:

The aim was, for each topic covered to report on the following questions:

What does it do?
What is the state of the art of this new technology? What can be done now? What is likely to be able to do be done soon (say in the next 3 years)? What technological or other developments make this possible?

What is the likely impact?
What is the potential impact of this development? What is likely to change? Which people will be most affected and how? Will this create, replace or make redundant any current jobs or technologies?

How will this affect you?
In your daily life, how will this affect you? What will be different for you? How might this affect members of your family or your friends?
